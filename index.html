Business Overview

Lynch Investigations & Countermeasures Pty Ltd operated as a licensed private investigation and security consultancy from 2003 to 2026. Based in Adelong in the Snowy Mountains foothills of New South Wales, the business provided services throughout the Riverina, South West Slopes, Canberra, Sydney, Melbourne, and internationally.

Under the direction of Principal Investigator Peter John Lynch, the business developed particular expertise in Technical Surveillance Counter-Measures (TSCM), serving over 200 TSCM clients with electronic sweep and physical inspection services using state-of-the-art equipment including spectrum analysis capability from DC to 75GHz, with 325GHz potential using harmonic mixers and horn antennas on client request.

This archive documents the services, capabilities, and professional achievements of the business over its 23-year operational history.
