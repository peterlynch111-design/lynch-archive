The Listening Post Series by Peter Lynch

The Listening Post was a series of articles authored by Peter Lynch covering Technical Surveillance Counter-Measures (TSCM). These articles were published to educate clients and the public about electronic surveillance threats, warning signs, and the professional debugging process.
