About Peter Lynch

Director and Principal of Lynch Investigations & Countermeasures Pty Ltd, providing professional investigation and security services across Australia for over two decades.

2003–2026 · Adelong, NSW · 300+ Clients Served

Professional Background

Peter Lynch established Lynch Investigations & Countermeasures Pty Ltd in 2003, building a comprehensive investigation and security consultancy that served more than 300 clients across Australia and internationally over its 23-year operational history.

The business combined traditional investigation services with specialist technical capabilities, most notably in TSCM (Technical Surveillance Counter-Measures) — the detection and neutralisation of covert surveillance devices. This dual capability, reflected in the company name "Investigations & Countermeasures," distinguished Lynch Investigations from generalist private investigation firms.

Operating from Adelong in the Snowy Mountains foothills of New South Wales, the business provided services throughout regional NSW, the ACT, Sydney, Melbourne, and interstate. Physical offices operated from Adelong, NSW and Sanctuary Lakes, VIC.

Service Philosophy

Lynch Investigations operated on principles of discretion, thoroughness, and evidence-based reporting. The business held two separate Master Licences under the Commercial Agents and Private Inquiry Agents Act 2004 (CAPI) in NSW: one for investigation work including process serving, locating persons, skip tracing, and debt collecting, and a Security Industry Licence for security consultancy. Victoria had its own licensing under the Private Security Act 2004 (Vic) — we held a Business Licence, Private Inquiry Agents Licence, and Security Consultant Licence with Victoria Police. CAPI was repealed on 1 July 2022. TSCM services are unregulated globally.

Reports and documentation were prepared to evidentiary standards suitable for legal proceedings, insurance claims, corporate governance, and regulatory compliance purposes. This attention to documentation quality reflected Peter Lynch's training in fraud investigation and forensic accounting disciplines.

TSCM Specialisation

The TSCM capability was the flagship service of Lynch Investigations, serving more than 200 clients over the business lifetime. Peter Lynch obtained TSCM certification from Countersurveillance Australia and the National Intelligence Research & Training Centre (NIRTC) in 2002, establishing technical capability before the business commenced formal operations.

Equipment investment included professional-grade detection systems from Research Electronics International (REI) including the OSCOR spectrum analyser, ORION non-linear junction detector, and CPM-700 broadband receiver. Extended frequency coverage to 75GHz (325GHz potential) was achieved through HP spectrum analysis equipment with harmonic mixers and horn antenna configurations.

Clients included legal practices protecting professional privilege, corporations safeguarding against industrial espionage, government contractors maintaining security clearances, and private individuals addressing domestic surveillance situations.
