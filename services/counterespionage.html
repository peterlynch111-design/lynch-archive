Counterespionage & Business Intelligence

Protecting businesses from corporate espionage, information theft, and competitive intelligence threats.

Corporate Espionage Threats

In today's competitive business environment, corporate espionage represents a significant threat to Australian businesses. Lynch Investigations & Countermeasures provided comprehensive counterespionage services to detect, prevent, and investigate information security breaches.

Counterespionage Services

  • TSCM Sweeps — Technical Surveillance Countermeasures to detect eavesdropping devices
  • Information Security Audits — Assessment of data protection measures
  • Leak Investigations — Identifying sources of confidential information breaches
  • Competitive Intelligence Analysis — Understanding competitor activities through ethical means
  • Security Awareness Training — Educating staff on espionage threats and prevention
  • Travel Security Briefings — Protecting executives and data during international travel

Business Intelligence Services

  • Market Intelligence — Ethical competitive research and analysis
  • Due Diligence — Background investigations on potential partners and acquisitions
  • Risk Assessment — Evaluating threats to business operations
  • Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) — Gathering publicly available information

Who is at Risk?

Any organisation with valuable intellectual property, trade secrets, competitive advantages, or sensitive client information may be targeted. Industries particularly at risk include technology, pharmaceuticals, defence contractors, legal firms, and financial services.
