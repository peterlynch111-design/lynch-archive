Product Sales — PJL Online Store

E-commerce operation selling investigation equipment, TSCM tools, surveillance gear, and reference materials to industry professionals and enthusiasts.

2003–2015 · 95 Products · Historical Service · No Longer Available

Service Overview

The PJL Online Store operated from 2003 to 2015, providing investigation and security equipment to professionals and enthusiasts throughout Australia. The store offered 95 products across multiple categories, from professional TSCM equipment to reference books and investigation tools.

TSCM equipment sales were discontinued in 2014-15 due to regulatory changes affecting the sale of surveillance detection equipment. The general product lines were wound down around the same time to focus on core investigation and TSCM services.

Notable Products

The store offered professional-grade equipment used by licensed investigators and security professionals. Notable product lines included:

  • Research Electronics International (REI): Professional TSCM equipment including OSCOR spectrum analysers, ORION non-linear junction detectors, and CPM receivers
  • Yukon Night Vision: Night vision monoculars and binoculars for surveillance applications
  • Reference Library: Technical publications on TSCM, fraud examination, investigation techniques, and security management
  • Voice Recorders: Professional-grade digital voice recorders for interview and surveillance applications

Discontinuation

The PJL Online Store was wound down during 2014-2015 for several reasons:

  • Regulatory changes: Changes affecting sale of surveillance detection equipment
  • Market evolution: Increased competition from larger online retailers
  • Focus: Decision to concentrate on core investigation and TSCM services
  • Efficiency: E-commerce operations required significant ongoing maintenance

The store closure allowed greater focus on the service-based investigation and TSCM work that formed the core business.
