Corporate Investigations

Comprehensive corporate security services addressing internal threats, fraud, theft, and workplace misconduct for Australian businesses.

Protecting Business Interests

Lynch Investigations & Countermeasures provided comprehensive corporate investigation services designed to protect business interests, assets, and reputation. With extensive experience in fraud examination and forensic accounting, we delivered thorough, court-admissible investigations.

Corporate Investigation Services

  • Fraud Investigation — Internal fraud, embezzlement, and financial irregularities
  • Employee Theft Investigation — Asset misappropriation and inventory shrinkage
  • Workplace Misconduct — Harassment, policy violations, and behavioural issues
  • Corporate Intelligence — Business intelligence and competitive analysis
  • Security Investigations — Security breach analysis and response
  • Risk Consultancy — Security risk assessment and management
  • Loss Prevention — Asset protection strategies and investigation
  • Computer Forensics — Digital evidence recovery and analysis

Investigation Methodology

All corporate investigations followed established forensic protocols ensuring evidence integrity and admissibility. Investigations were conducted with appropriate discretion to minimise workplace disruption while maintaining thoroughness.

Qualifications

Principal investigator Peter Lynch held qualifications in Fraud Examination and Forensic Accounting, along with certifications including CISM, CISA, and CISSP. This combination of investigative experience and technical expertise enabled comprehensive corporate security solutions.
