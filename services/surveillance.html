Surveillance Services

Professional surveillance operations supporting insurance investigations, litigation, family law matters, and corporate security throughout Australia.

2003–2026 · Lynch Investigations & Countermeasures

Service Overview

Surveillance was a core investigation service, providing evidential observation and documentation of subject activities. All surveillance operations were conducted within legal frameworks, with comprehensive reporting suitable for court proceedings, insurance claims, and corporate decision-making.

Operating from the Riverina region provided particular expertise in rural surveillance environments where traditional urban techniques require adaptation. The regional base also enabled competitive positioning for surveillance work throughout country NSW, the ACT, and into Victoria.

Surveillance Types

Static Surveillance

Fixed position observation of premises, monitoring subject arrivals, departures, visitors, and activities. Suitable for residential addresses, workplaces, and locations of interest. Extended duration capability with comprehensive logging.

Mobile Surveillance

Vehicle-based observation following subjects through urban and regional areas. Documented travel patterns, destinations, meetings, and activities. Single and multi-vehicle operations depending on requirements.

Rural Surveillance

Specialist observation in farming, regional, and remote areas where traditional urban surveillance techniques are ineffective. Adapted methods for low-traffic environments, large properties, and agricultural settings.

Foot Surveillance

On-foot observation in shopping centres, CBD areas, public events, and locations inaccessible by vehicle. Documented activities, meetings, purchases, and interactions.

Night Surveillance

After-hours observation using appropriate equipment and techniques for low-light conditions. Night vision capability for documenting nocturnal activities.

Satellite / GPS Tracking

Vehicle tracking using GPS technology where legally authorised. Real-time and historical location data supporting surveillance operations and establishing movement patterns.

Applications

Insurance Claims

Workers compensation surveillance documenting claimant activities inconsistent with claimed injuries. Property and liability claim verification. Evidence for claim determination and fraud investigation.

Family Law

Evidence gathering for custody disputes, property settlement matters, and relationship investigations. Documentation of parenting arrangements, lifestyle, and associations.

Litigation Support

Surveillance evidence for civil and criminal proceedings. Witness location and observation, defendant activity documentation, and evidence preservation.

Corporate

Employee misconduct investigations, theft prevention, policy compliance verification, and competitive intelligence gathering within legal boundaries.

Documentation & Reporting

All surveillance operations produced comprehensive documentation suitable for evidentiary purposes:

  • Surveillance logs: Timestamped activity records with location details
  • Photographic evidence: Date/time stamped imagery documenting observations
  • Video evidence: Motion footage where circumstances permitted
  • Written reports: Narrative summaries prepared to evidentiary standards
  • Court attendance: Expert witness availability for proceedings

Geographic Coverage

Surveillance services were provided throughout:

  • Primary regions: Riverina NSW, South West Slopes, Snowy Mountains, Canberra ACT
  • Extended coverage: Sydney, Wollongong, Melbourne
  • Interstate: All Australian states and territories

The regional base provided cost-effective coverage for country NSW and ACT work, with competitive travel costs compared to metropolitan-based operators.
