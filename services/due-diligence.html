Corporate Due Diligence

Experienced professional investigators produce the answers to your vital questions.

Informed Decision Making

Before entering into any agreements or transactions, you should be fully informed about the risks so you can manage risks and avoid unpleasant surprises. Lynch Investigations & Countermeasures provided comprehensive due diligence services for law firms, corporations, and investors.

Due Diligence Services

  • Corporate History & Ownership — Research of corporate history and ownership structure
  • Criminal & Civil Background — Litigation background research and court record searches
  • Fraud Examination — Forensic accounting and fraud detection
  • Financial Disclosure Audits — Verification of financial statements and disclosures
  • Security & Control Audits — Internal security and control assessments
  • Liability Searches — Locating unstated legal or financial liabilities
  • Media Research — Comprehensive media searches for relevant details or events
  • Discreet Interviews — Formal or discreet interviews with knowledgeable parties
  • Business Intelligence — Local or overseas business intelligence gathering
  • Associate Searches — Locating prior co-workers, business partners, and associates
