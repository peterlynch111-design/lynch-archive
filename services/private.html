Private Investigations

Personal investigation services for individuals and families addressing domestic concerns, relationship matters, missing persons, and private security issues.

2003–2026 · Lynch Investigations & Countermeasures

Service Overview

Private investigation services addressed the personal concerns of individuals and families — matters too sensitive or complex for self-investigation, yet not appropriate for police involvement. These investigations required particular discretion and sensitivity to personal circumstances.

All private investigations were conducted within legal frameworks, with clear communication about what could and could not be lawfully investigated. Reports were prepared to standards suitable for family court proceedings where required.

Services Provided

Family Law Matters

Evidence gathering for family court proceedings including property settlement, parenting arrangements, and relationship matters. Documentation of lifestyle, living conditions, and compliance with court orders.

Child Custody Investigations

Investigation supporting custody and parenting disputes. Documentation of parenting capacity, living conditions, associations, and child welfare concerns. Evidence for family court proceedings.

Missing Persons / Locate

Location of missing family members, estranged relatives, old friends, and other persons. Skip tracing, database searches, and discreet enquiries to establish current whereabouts.

Background Investigations

Investigation of new partners, business associates, and persons of interest. Verification of representations and identification of concerning background information.

Relationship Investigations

Discreet investigation of relationship concerns. Surveillance and enquiries to establish facts where suspicions exist. Sensitive handling of personal matters.

Domestic Surveillance

TSCM sweeps for private residences where technical surveillance is suspected. Detection of listening devices, hidden cameras, and GPS trackers in domestic situations.

Additional Services

Heir Location

Location of beneficiaries and potential heirs for estate matters. Tracing family members who may be entitled to inheritance or need to be notified of estate proceedings.

Witness Location

Finding witnesses for legal proceedings, accident reconstructions, and historical matters. Location and preliminary contact for legal representatives.

Debtor Location

Skip tracing for individuals owing money. Current address verification and asset identification supporting debt recovery efforts.

Process Serving

Personal service of legal documents including court orders, subpoenas, and statutory notices. Affidavit of service provided for court filing.

Approach

Private investigations required particular care in handling sensitive personal matters:

  • Discretion: All investigations conducted with absolute confidentiality
  • Legal compliance: Clear advice on what could be lawfully investigated
  • Sensitivity: Recognition of emotional aspects of personal investigations
  • Documentation: Reports suitable for court proceedings where required
  • Communication: Regular updates and clear explanation of findings
