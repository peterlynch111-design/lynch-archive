Factual Investigations

Professional fact-finding investigations supporting legal proceedings and dispute resolution.

Evidence-Based Investigation

Factual investigations involve the systematic gathering of evidence and information to establish the facts of a matter. Lynch Investigations & Countermeasures provided professional factual investigation services supporting solicitors, barristers, insurance companies, and corporate clients.

Factual Investigation Services

  • Witness Statements — Obtaining detailed statements from witnesses
  • Witness Location — Tracing and locating witnesses for legal matters
  • Scene Examination — Attending and documenting accident or incident scenes
  • Evidence Collection — Gathering physical and documentary evidence
  • Photographic Documentation — Professional photographic evidence
  • Interview Conduct — Structured interviews with relevant parties
  • Document Retrieval — Obtaining relevant documents and records
  • Timeline Construction — Establishing chronology of events

Legal Support

Factual investigations were conducted to support various legal matters including:

  • Civil litigation
  • Criminal defence preparation
  • Personal injury claims
  • Motor vehicle accidents
  • Workers compensation matters
  • Workplace incidents
  • Property disputes
  • Contractual disputes

Court-Ready Evidence

All factual investigations were conducted with the understanding that findings may be required for court proceedings. Evidence was gathered and documented in accordance with evidentiary requirements, and the principal investigator was available to provide expert testimony when required.
