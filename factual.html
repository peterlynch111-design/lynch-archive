Factual Investigations

Evidence-based investigation establishing facts for legal proceedings, regulatory matters, and dispute resolution with documentation to evidentiary standards.

2003–2026 · Lynch Investigations & Countermeasures

Service Overview

Factual investigation services gathered and documented evidence for matters proceeding to legal determination — court proceedings, tribunal hearings, disciplinary processes, and dispute resolution forums. The focus was on establishing provable facts rather than opinions or assessments.

All documentation was prepared to evidentiary standards with appropriate chain of custody, authentication, and witness availability for proceedings.

Services Provided

Criminal Defence

Independent investigation supporting criminal defence matters. Witness location and interview, alibi verification, scene examination, and evidence review. Working with defence solicitors and barristers.

Fraud Investigation

Comprehensive fraud investigation with specialist qualifications in fraud examination. Evidence gathering for civil recovery, criminal referral, and regulatory action. Financial analysis and document examination.

Arson Investigation

Independent investigation of suspicious fires. Scene examination (post fire service), witness interviews, and background investigation. Evidence for insurance claims and criminal proceedings.

Burglary & Theft

Investigation of theft and burglary matters beyond police investigation. Recovery of stolen property, identification of perpetrators, and evidence for civil recovery.

Stolen Motor Vehicle

Investigation of motor vehicle theft including insurance fraud indicators, vehicle location efforts, and evidence for disputed claims.

Counterfeit Consumer Goods

Investigation of counterfeit product distribution. Evidence gathering for brand owners, identification of distribution networks, and support for enforcement action.

Internet Investigations

Specialist capability in online investigation addressing matters occurring in digital spaces:

  • Online fraud: Investigation of internet-based fraud schemes and perpetrator identification
  • Defamation: Evidence preservation and source identification for online defamation
  • Harassment: Documentation of online harassment and stalking for legal proceedings
  • Intellectual property: Identification of online IP infringement and evidence collection
  • Social media: Evidence preservation from social media platforms

Evidence Standards

Factual investigations produced documentation meeting evidentiary requirements:

  • Chain of custody: Documented handling of physical and electronic evidence
  • Authentication: Verification and documentation of evidence authenticity
  • Witness statements: Signed statements prepared to court requirements
  • Expert reports: Written reports suitable for tendering in proceedings
  • Court attendance: Availability as witness for examination and cross-examination

Relevant Qualifications

Factual investigation services were supported by specialist qualifications:

  • Post Graduate Certificate in Fraud Investigation — Charles Sturt University (2007)
  • Advanced Diploma of Government Fraud Control Management — Australian Forensic Services (2005)
  • Crime Scene Investigation 101 — Universal Class (2011)
  • Association of Certified Fraud Examiners (ACFE) membership
  • Justice of the Peace (NSW) — document certification and statutory declarations
